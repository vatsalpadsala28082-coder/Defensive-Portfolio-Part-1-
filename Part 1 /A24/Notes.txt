A24 – Teach Family About Cybersecurity

Topic: Scam and Phishing Awareness

What is Phishing:
Phishing is a type of online scam where attackers send fake emails or messages to trick users into sharing personal information such as passwords or OTPs.

Common Scam Examples:
- Fake bank messages
- Delivery or parcel scams
- Emails saying your account will be blocked
- Fake alerts from Microsoft or Google

Warning Signs:
- Unknown or suspicious sender
- Urgent or threatening language
- Suspicious or shortened links
- Spelling or grammar mistakes

How to Stay Safe:
- Do not click on unknown links
- Always check the sender email address
- Use official apps or websites instead of message links
- Never share OTP, passwords, or personal information

Important Rule:
If a message creates urgency or fear, do not trust it immediately. Verify it first.
