
REFLECTION


For this activity, I taught a family member about cybersecurity focusing on scam messages and phishing attacks. I explained how attackers send fake emails or messages that appear to be from trusted companies like banks or Microsoft in order to steal personal information.

During the session, my family member was surprised to learn how realistic scam messages can be and mentioned that they had received similar messages before but did not realise they could be dangerous. They showed interest and asked questions about how to identify fake messages and what actions to take.

After the discussion, they understood the importance of checking links, verifying the sender, and not sharing sensitive information such as passwords or OTPs. This activity helped me improve my communication skills and made me realise how important cybersecurity awareness is for everyday users.
